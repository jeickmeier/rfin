Prerequisites
-------------

Before starting these tutorials, you should:

* Complete all :doc:`../beginner/index` tutorials
* Understand basic derivatives pricing concepts
* Be comfortable with Polars/Pandas DataFrames
* Have experience with portfolio management concepts

Tutorial Overview
-----------------

1. **Exotic Options** - Barrier, Asian, lookback options with analytical and MC pricing
2. **Credit Default Swaps** - CDS pricing, spreads, and credit curves
3. **Curve Calibration** - Bootstrap curves from market quotes
4. **Scenario Analysis** - Advanced stress testing and composition
5. **Portfolio Optimization** - Constrained portfolio construction
6. **Risk Metrics** - DV01, CS01, Greeks, bucketed risk
7. **FX Conversion** - Multi-currency portfolios with FX policies
8. **Custom Instruments** - Extend finstack with your own instrument types

Learning Outcomes
-----------------

After completing these tutorials, you will be able to:

* Price and risk-manage complex derivative instruments
* Calibrate market data from quotes
* Build and stress-test multi-asset portfolios
* Optimize portfolio allocations with constraints
* Handle multi-currency positions with explicit FX
* Extend finstack for custom use cases

Time Investment
---------------

Each tutorial takes approximately **30-60 minutes**. Total time: ~6-8 hours.
