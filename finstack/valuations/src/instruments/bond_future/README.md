# Bond Future

Bond futures are standardized exchange-traded contracts to buy or sell a basket of eligible bonds at a specified future date.

## Overview

This module implements bond futures (e.g., UST Treasury futures, German Bund futures, UK Gilt futures) with support for:
- Deliverable basket mechanics with conversion factors
- Cheapest-to-deliver (CTD) bond selection
- Invoice price calculation at settlement
- Contract DV01 and bucketed risk metrics
